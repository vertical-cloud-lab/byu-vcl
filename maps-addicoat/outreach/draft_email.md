Congratulations on launching Addicoat. We have been following the MAPS work
(starting from the *CIRP Annals* 2022 paper through the recent *Additive

I lead the Vertical Cloud Lab at BYU, where we run automated, high‑throughput

1. Where are you today on roll‑to‑roll / DED versus cut‑and‑sinter routes, and

3. What feedstock characterization do you provide with the sheets (e.g.

   joint characterization?
4. What does an early evaluation engagement look like — sample sheets, a paid
   pilot, or a co‑development project?

We are happy to share more about our automated screening setup and how it could
accelerate composition development for your sheets. Would a short call in the
coming weeks suit you?
