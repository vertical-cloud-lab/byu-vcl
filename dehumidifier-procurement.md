## Key Features to Consider

### For Laboratory Environments:
1. **MERV-13 Filtration** (Quest 155, Santa Fe Ultra V155) - Essential for maintaining clean air in labs
2. **Energy Efficiency** - Higher pints/kWh reduces operating costs
3. **Digital Controls** - Precise humidity setpoint management
4. **Continuous Drainage** - Essential for 24/7 operation
5. **Auto-Restart** - Maintains operation after power interruptions
6. **Low Noise Operation** - Important for occupied lab spaces
7. **Temperature Range** - Should operate reliably at lab temperatures
8. **Warranty** - 5-year warranty preferred for institutional use

## Installation Considerations

1. **Drainage**: All units require proper condensate drainage. Options include:
   - Gravity drain to floor drain
   - Condensate pump for elevated drainage
   - Connection to building drainage system

2. **Electrical**: Verify adequate 115-120V electrical service at installation location

3. **Airflow**: Ensure adequate clearance for air intake/exhaust (typically 12-18" minimum)

4. **Ducting** (if applicable): Santa Fe and Aprilaire models support ducted installations for distributed dehumidification

5. **Placement**: Consider proximity to:
   - Sensitive equipment requiring humidity control
   - Electrical outlets
   - Drainage access points
   - Air circulation patterns in the lab
